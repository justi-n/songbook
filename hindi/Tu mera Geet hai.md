# Tu mera Geet hai
```
Tu mera geet hai
Mera sangeet hai
Jeevan ki raah mein
Tu meet hai

Karun ab teri araadhana
Sampurna man se upaasana
De mujhko apni aatma mein yaha

Mein yaha Prabhu (x3)
Mein hu yaha

Tu meri aasha
Tu mera preet hai
Jeevan ki haar mein
Tu jeet hai
```
